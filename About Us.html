About Virtuous Gold Mining


The Leading Gold Mining Company

Virtuous Gold Mining LLC is a Limited Liability Company in Ghana that has been trading in Precious metals and minerals since its inception in August, 2012. We have been able to legally secure various mining concessions to exploit, all of which are approved by the Government of the Republic of Ghana.

We source Precious metals and minerals for our cherished clients under the Laws of Ghana regulating our main operations.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best and environmentally friendly mining practices and quality products for our high profile clients.


Our Vision

Our vision is to become the biggest Gold mining and sourcing company in Ghana and Africa.


Our Values

The core values of Virtuous Gold Mining LLC are Discipline, Respect and Integrity. We hold these values in high esteem and that is what makes us one of the most reputable Precious minerals mining company in Ghana.


Sustainable Environmental Practices

Virtuous Gold Mining LLC deems it a strategic and corporate responsibility to ensure its supports for legal and sound extracting practices to promote sustainable development. The company takes into account the importance of making use of every raw material and operating under friendly, safe and healthy conditions.
